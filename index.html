為什麼許多學生卡在 Band 5-6？ Why do many students get stuck at Band 5-6?

許多補習班、線上 IELTS 套裝課程專注於： Many IELTS courses focus on:

  • 題型分析 Question-type analysis
  • 考試技巧 Test-taking techniques
  • 高分模板 High-score templates
  • 大量刷題 Large amounts of drilling

這些方法都像止痛藥一樣，只能抑制表面病徵，無法根除病因。5-6分的成績反映出學生在處理複雜資訊、即時組織想法和表達個人觀點方面有困難，這些都源自於基礎能力的不足，如果沒有針對性地訓練這些能力，就無法突破瓶頸。 These methods are like painkillers: they may suppress surface symptoms, but they cannot remove the root cause. Band 5-6 often reflects difficulty processing complex information, organizing ideas in real time, and expressing personal viewpoints. These issues come from gaps in core ability, and without targeted training, it is hard to break through the bottleneck.

就像看醫生一樣 Like seeing a doctor

一直頭痛時，好的醫生不會只是不斷開止痛藥。他會先找出睡眠、壓力、眼睛、姿勢或更深層的原因。 When someone has constant headaches, a good doctor does not only prescribe more painkillers. They first check sleep, stress, eyes, posture, and deeper causes.

我不只是告訴學生「這題答案是什麼」。我更重視為什麼會錯，以及真正卡住的是哪一個能力。 I do not only tell students what the answer is. I focus on why the mistake happened and which ability is blocking progress.

Reading | 閱讀

閱讀錯誤不一定只是看不懂英文。 Reading mistakes are not always just about not understanding English.

先檢查單字量是否不足？Is vocabulary the bottleneck?
再訓練建立單字學習方法，讓單字真的能用在理解文意。Build a vocabulary method that supports real text comprehension.
先檢查文法基礎是否不穩？Is grammar unstable?
再訓練歸納理解文法，讀懂句子裡每個元素間的關係。Strengthen grammar so sentence relationships become clear.
先檢查長句字太多，看了又看十幾遍還是不懂？Are long sentences hard to break down?
再訓練練習解構技能，先抓主幹，再看修飾與補充資訊。Practice long-sentence breakdown: main structure first, details second.
先檢查看得懂句子，卻推不出答案？Do you understand the sentence but miss the answer logic?
再訓練重點資訊判斷，並一步一步推理出暗示什麼。Train key-point judgment and step-by-step answer reasoning.
先檢查沒有掌握略讀、預測、掃讀的方法？Are skimming, predicting, and scanning unclear?
再訓練用略讀找方向、預測文章邏輯、掃讀找細節。Use skimming for direction, prediction for logic, and scanning for detail.

Listening | 聽力

聽力練很久卻沒進步，常常是方法不對。 Long listening practice without progress often means the method is not working.

先檢查單字看得懂，卻聽不出來？Can you read words but not recognize them by ear?
再訓練把聽力和單字一起練，建立聲音與意思的連結。Train listening with vocabulary to connect sound and meaning.
先檢查不熟悉英文的連音和弱音？Are linking and weak forms unfamiliar?
再訓練練習跟讀，改善語音辨識與節奏感。Use shadowing to improve sound recognition and rhythm.
先檢查聽得到字，卻抓不到重點？Do you hear words but miss the main point?
再訓練學習聽重點，而不是每個字都想聽懂。Train listening for key points instead of every single word.
先檢查太依賴字幕？Do you rely too much on subtitles?
再訓練把聽力和口說一起練，讓耳朵與嘴巴一起建立反應。Connect listening with speaking so recognition becomes active.
先檢查平常練習方式沒有效果？Is your regular practice ineffective?
再訓練練習整理筆記，建立適合自己的日常聽力訓練方法。Practice note organization and build a personal daily listening routine.

Writing | 寫作

寫作卡住常常不是文法問題，而是不知道內容如何展開。 Writing problems are often not only grammar problems. The bigger issue is developing content.

先檢查看不懂題目真正要問什麼？Do you misread what the prompt is asking?
再訓練訓練審題，先判斷題目要求與回應方向。Train prompt analysis to identify the task and response direction.
先檢查想不到自己的觀點？Is it hard to form your own position?
再訓練從關鍵字出發，並把簡單想法寫得更完整。Practice forming a view and developing simple ideas more fully.
先檢查理由太薄弱？Are your reasons too thin?
再訓練漸進式揭露細節，讓論點更有說服力。Reveal details progressively so your arguments become more persuasive.
先檢查例子不夠清楚？Are your examples unclear?
再訓練區分細節程度，避免過於模糊。Control the level of detail so examples do not stay vague.
先檢查段落安排混亂？Is paragraph organization confusing?
再訓練繪製邏輯關聯圖，讓文章前後連貫。Map the logical connections so the essay stays coherent from paragraph to paragraph.
先檢查英文句子限制了想法表達？Do English sentences limit your ideas?
再訓練按照英文句型結構來表達想法。Use English sentence structures to express ideas directly.

Speaking | 口說

口說不流利，不一定是英文太差，可能是想法和組織方式卡住。 Speaking fluency is not always about weak English. Ideas and organization may be the bottleneck.

先檢查單字和常用語句不夠？Are vocabulary and useful phrases limited?
再訓練能快速內化的學習方法。Use learning methods that help you internalize phrases quickly.
先檢查不知道怎麼延伸回答？Do you struggle to extend answers?
再訓練解釋原因、舉例，讓回答自然變長。Practice explaining reasons and giving examples in English.
先檢查不習慣表達自己的想法？Are you unused to expressing your own ideas?
再訓練練習比較不同觀點，並在討論中自然回應。Practice comparing viewpoints and responding naturally in discussion.
先檢查太依賴中文翻譯？Do you depend too much on Chinese translation?
再訓練將單字關聯性融入單字學習。Build vocabulary by connecting words through relationships and associations.
先檢查遇到陌生題目就緊張？Do unfamiliar questions make you nervous?
再訓練如何將陌生資訊與過去經驗連結Learn to connect unfamiliar information with past experience.
先檢查平常缺少真正互動練習？Do you lack real interaction practice?
再訓練找到把練習融入日常生活的辦法。Find ways to make practice part of daily life.

我想培養的，不只是 IELTS 分數 What I want to build is more than an IELTS score

IELTS 成績很重要。但真正穩定的進步，不是來自無止盡刷題，而是來自找對問題，並一步一步把能力補起來。 The IELTS score matters. But stable progress does not come from endless drilling. It comes from finding the right problem and rebuilding the ability step by step.
